# Historical Figures

This directory contains detailed biographies of significant historical figures from the canon.

## Figures by Country

### Aerobea

#### Presidents

- **[Barkley Thunderflap](barkley-thunderflap.md)** (1766-1830, dog) - President 1818-1821, first non-human president, GSC party
- **[Commander Nullglyph](commander-nullglyph.md)** (1717-1797) - President across three terms (1761-1777, 1777-1779, 1785-1787), survived coup attempt, Whig party
- **[Emory Di Marison](emory-di-marison.md)** (1720-1778) - Radical leader who seized power April-September 1777, executed by burning, Radical party
- **[Feathery Quill](feathery-quill.md)** (1791-1821, owl) - President for six days in November 1821, first avian president, died in blimp crash, Feather First party

### Aurob

#### Prime Ministers

- **[Cluckminster Pexly](cluckminster-pexly.md)** (1970-present, chicken) - Prime Minister 2006-2025, led progressive technocratic reforms, Labour party
- **[Defern Storkland](defern-storkland.md)** (1965-present, stork) - Current Prime Minister 2025-present, populist-progressive government, Labour party
- **[Feather Owlquol](feather-owlquol.md)** (1926-2025, owl) - Prime Minister 1990-2006, security-focused government, Moderate Nationalist party
- **[Lord Cathnater](lord-cathnater.md)** (1894-1966) - Prime Minister 1932-1954, longest-serving PM, authoritarian industrial expansion, Nationalist party
- **[Pellow Doveheart](pellow-doveheart.md)** (1925-2025) - Prime Minister in two terms (1954-1964, 1969-1976), democratic reforms, Labour party
- **[Quillian Featherstone](quillian-featherstone.md)** (1920-1990) - Prime Minister 1964-1969, technocratic stabilization, Moderate Nationalist party
- **[Yukoi Jason](yukoi-jason.md)** (1936-2025) - Prime Minister 1976-1990, industrial modernization, Labour party
