# Consistency Notes and Historical Cross-References

This document tracks key historical facts, cross-references between documents, and notes any inconsistencies that need resolution.

## Purpose

The post-war timeline (1947-2025) was created to show how fictional nations interfaced with real-world events. This document ensures all historical details align across country files, event files, and the comprehensive timeline.

- **Historical Foundation**: Lord Cathnater's industrial expansion 1932-1954

- **Contemporary**: "Learning Republic" education system, green technology, social democracy

- **Philosophy**: Fish and Curiosity Party's "quality of life over GDP growth"

- **Traditional**: Dairy exports ("Smart Milk" brand), wool products

- **Traditional**: Premium wool ("Ethical Fleece" brand), sustainable practices

### Iuyusoy: Manufacturing/Services
- **Foundation**: Colonial Scroll-Merit System (efficient bureaucracy)

- **Core Belief**: Record-keeping and knowledge preservation as sacred civic duty
- **Motto**: "To record is to reconcile" (Aerobea)
- **Applications**: Digital governance, data ethics, bureaucratic efficiency
- **Influence**: Shaped Fog-See Alliance values of transparency and accountability

### Pasture Philosophy (Nitopia)
- **Framework**: Democracy, environmental stewardship, interspecies cooperation
- **Application**: Data ethics, AI governance, sustainable agriculture
- **Global Impact**: Influenced EU GDPR, tech ethics worldwide

### Curiosity and Mischief (Catland)

- **Economic**: "Sufficiency economy" over growth-at-all-costs
