## International Organizations

### Fog-See Alliance
Founded in 1954, the Fog-See Alliance is the interspecies confederation of post-Scrollist republics.

**Founding Members:**
- Aerobea (headquarters in Peaville)
- Nitopia
- Catland
- Moohfalanda
- Iuyusoy

**Key Achievements:**
- Fog-See Peace of Pastures (1975) - Ended the Cattle–Wool Trade Dispute
- Fog-See Cultural League - Promoting interspecies art and diplomacy
- Fog-See Navigation Network - Joint operation by Aerobea and Nitopia
- Fog-See Secretariat - Located in Peaville, Aerobea

## Major Treaties & Accords

### Fog-Health Accord (1795/1861)
Medical diplomacy agreement between Aerobea and Nitopia following the Pasture Fever Era. Created jointly by Aerobean doctors and Nitopian engineers.

### Feather-Fog Accord (1954)
Post-war reconciliation treaty between Aerobea (President Avae Romrowabala) and Aurob (Prime Minister Pellow Doveheart), marking the end of ideological tensions from the 1940s Fog-See wars.

### Fog-Trough Accords
Brokered by Nitopian President Lossa Milco (1934-1947).

### Arribin Education Compact
Open research programs and educational exchange between Aerobea and Iuyusoy.

### Peaville–Fogvale Fellowship
Cultural exchange program between Aerobea and Moohfalanda.

## Historical Context

### Diplomatic Eras

**Runway Republic Era (1701–1800)**
- Focus: Expansion & Codification
- Establishing administrative colonies (Iuyusoy, early Moohfalanda)

**Industrial Fog Age (1800–1900)**
- Focus: Education Diplomacy
- Exporting universities and Scrollist law

**Post-War Era (1900–1970)**
- Focus: Reconstruction & Alliance
- Founding the Fog-See Alliance (1954)
- Reconciliation with Aurob

**Digital Pasture Age (1970–Present)**
- Focus: Knowledge Integration
- Promoting open data, environmental reform, and cultural unity
