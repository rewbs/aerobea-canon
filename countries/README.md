# Countries

This directory contains detailed information about the countries in the canon.

## Country List

### [Aerobea](aerobea.md)
**Capital:** To be determined
**Founded:** 1672
**Location:** Island nation in European waters
**Government:** Presidential republic

Island nation known for its rich political history spanning from 1672 to the present. Notable for its diverse leadership including multiple political parties and historic non-human presidents (GSC and Feather First parties). The country has experienced periods of stability, radical upheaval (including the 1777 Marison Coup), and continuous democratic evolution through 34 presidents.

### [Aurob](aurob.md)
**Capital:** To be determined
**Founded:** 21 February 1894
**Location:** European mainland
**Government:** Parliamentary democracy

Parliamentary nation known for its industrial development and progressive labor movements. Experienced a 22-year period of authoritarian industrial expansion under Lord Cathnater (1932-1954), followed by democratic consolidation. Notable for acceptance of non-human political leadership (owls, chickens, storks). Currently led by Prime Minister Defern Storkland (2025-present).

---

### [Catland](catland.md)
**Capital:** To be determined
**Founded:** 31 December 1726
**Location:** European region
**Government:** Presidential republic

Presidential republic with nearly 300 years of democratic tradition, dominated by two major parties: the Fish and Curiosity Party and the Mischief Party. Known for long presidential terms and exceptional political stability. Notable for the tragic 1905 Quadruple Silence and the Falmone era economic crisis. Currently led by President Peanutboltbolt (2023-present).

---

### [Moohfalanda](moohfalanda.md)
**Capital:** To be determined
**Founded:** 1 January 1870
**Location:** To be determined
**Government:** Presidential republic

Presidential republic with diverse political history spanning over 150 years. Known for both stable long-term administrations and periods of rapid turnover. Notable for the Mooson political dynasty and Edward Jones's unique four separate presidential terms spanning 1957-1995. Currently led by President David Harris (2022-present).

---

### [Nitopia](nitopia.md)
**Capital:** Needles
**Founded:** 1560 (as kingdom), 1843 (as republic)
**Location:** To be determined
**Government:** Constitutional republic

Constitutional republic originally founded as a kingdom in 1560, known for its wool-based economy and unique fog-related culture. Transitioned to democratic republic in 1843 while maintaining ceremonial monarchy. Features distinctive scroll-based bureaucracy, fleece trade traditions, and acceptance of non-human leaders. Currently led by President Andy Nittalot (first donkey president, 2023-present).
