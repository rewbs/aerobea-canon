# Aurob

## Overview

Aurob is a parliamentary nation founded on 21 February 1894, known for its industrial development, progressive labor movements, and unique acceptance of non-human political leadership. The country operates as a constitutional democracy with a Prime Minister as head of government, and has experienced a complex political history balancing nationalist industrial growth with progressive labor reforms.

### Political Parties

#### Nationalist Party
Founded in Aurob's early years, the Nationalist Party traditionally advocates for industrial development, economic nationalism, and strong national identity. The party evolved from Lord Cathnater's authoritarian approach to the more moderate, technocratic governance of later leaders like Quillian Featherstone.

**Key figures:** Lord Cathnater, Quillian Featherstone, Feather Owlquol

#### Labour Party
The Labour Party represents workers, unions, and progressive social policies. Has held power for the majority of Aurob's democratic era (post-1954), implementing welfare programs, worker protections, and progressive reforms.

**Key figures:** Pellow Doveheart, Yukoi Jason, Cluckminster Pexly, Defern Storkland

#### Moderate Nationalist Party
A centrist offshoot of the original Nationalist Party, focused on technocratic governance, balanced economic policies, and democratic stability. Represents the middle ground between nationalist economics and progressive social policies.

**Key figures:** Quillian Featherstone, Feather Owlquol
